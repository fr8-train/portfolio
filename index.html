About me

Full name: Noah michael Hansen Nickname:fr8.train

Currently I am a 17 year old junior from Maple valley Jr./sr. high school, and also study digital media and design through the Eaton RESA Career Prep Program.  I have always liked the thought of making things that people will like. Movies have inspired me my entire life and have led me to an interest in digital design and marketing. It's just so cool to me how you can take the things that you see in your imagination and turn them into a reality through the power of technology. aside from the artsy fartsy stuff, i enjoy things on the complete opposite end of the hobbyist spectrum. I am a three-sport athlete, playing basketball, football, and baseball every year in high school, being moved up onto varsity for football my sophomore year. My favorite of the three sports is arguably the least popular, baseball. nothing beats a good day of good baseball. I love hunting and fishing and would consider myself somewhat of an outdoorsman  (quite shocking if you just look at me from the outside-in i would say). I have an amazing kindergarten teacher of a mom, and an awesome digital media manager of a Dad. Both of them are incredible and have supported me every step of the way. I also have a cool sister named Kayli, and a Chocolate lab named after a Hawaiian island, Maui. In the future I look to attend a 4-year university and major in marketing, while minoring in digital media.
